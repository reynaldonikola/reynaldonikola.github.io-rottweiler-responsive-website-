Why people admire Rottweilers

Rottweilers are powerful, dependable dogs with a long working history. They are admired for their courage, intelligence, and loyalty to their families. With proper training and daily activity they become calm and steady companions.

Protective nature

Rottweilers are naturally confident and protective. They build strong bonds with their families.

Training and exercise

They respond well to consistent training, positive reinforcement and daily mental stimulation.

Care and grooming

Their coat is easy to maintain but regular brushing and healthy nutrition are important.

Loyal companion

When raised properly, Rottweilers are affectionate, stable and loyal family dogs.
